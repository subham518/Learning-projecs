The village elders of Odisha speak of an ancient forest believed to be protected by spirits.
It is said that travelers who enter the forest with pure intentions are guided safely.
Local folklore tells of a hidden shrine built by unknown tribes centuries ago.
During festivals, villagers offer prayers to honor the guardians of the land.
Stories passed orally describe how the forest once saved the village from a great drought.
Elders believe the spirits still watch over the people and the forest remains sacred.
